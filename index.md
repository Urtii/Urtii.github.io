I am a PhD student and Research Assistant in the
[Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering]({{ site.author.department_url }})
at [Hacettepe University](https://www.hacettepe.edu.tr/english/),
advised by [Assoc. Prof. İsmail Uyanık](https://www.ee.hacettepe.edu.tr/?link=400999&sublink=280&lang=e)
and a member of [NeuRoLab](https://neurolab.ee.hacettepe.edu.tr/).
My PhD is supported by the TÜBİTAK 2211-A National PhD Scholarship Programme.

My research spans two interconnected areas:
**LTP system identification** — where I work on Floquet theory, harmonic
transfer functions, and periodic system analysis — and **robotics** — where I
previously focused on LiDAR-based odometry, 3D SLAM, and sensor fusion for
quadruped platforms. Alongside these, I contribute to neuromechanics research
studying the sensorimotor dynamics of weakly electric fish.

I completed my M.S. at Hacettepe University in February 2025, with a thesis on
sensor fusion and mapping using 3D LiDAR odometry on a quadruped robot platform.
